# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

At Naxus, we are committed to fostering a professional, respectful, and productive environment for all team members, partners, and clients involved with Unicorn HRMS.

## Our Standards

### Expected Behavior

- Maintain professional communication at all times
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accept constructive criticism gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the project and organization
- Show empathy and consideration towards others
- Protect confidential and proprietary information
- Follow company policies and procedures

### Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or intimidation of any kind
- Unprofessional or disrespectful language
- Sharing proprietary or confidential information without authorization
- Deliberate disruption of work processes
- Any conduct that violates company policies
- Unauthorized access to systems or data

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all Naxus team members, contractors, partners, and anyone interacting with the Unicorn HRMS project in professional contexts, including:

- Development environments
- Code reviews and technical discussions
- Project management and planning meetings
- Client interactions and support
- Company communication channels
- Professional social media interactions

## Enforcement

### Reporting

Violations of this Code of Conduct should be reported to:
- Email: conduct@naxus.com
- HR Department: hr@naxus.com
- Direct Manager or Team Lead

All reports will be handled with confidentiality and appropriate seriousness.

### Consequences

Violations may result in:
1. Verbal or written warning
2. Temporary suspension from project
3. Permanent removal from project
4. Termination of employment or contract
5. Legal action if warranted

The response will be proportionate to the severity and frequency of violations.
