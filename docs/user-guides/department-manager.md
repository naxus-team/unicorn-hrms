# Department Manager Guide

## Overview

As a Department Manager, you can manage your team's attendance, approve leave requests, conduct performance reviews, and access team reports.

## Dashboard

Your dashboard displays:
- Team attendance status
- Pending leave requests
- Upcoming performance reviews
- Team performance metrics
- Important notifications

## Team Management

### Viewing Your Team

1. Navigate to **My Team**
2. View all direct reports
3. See team hierarchy
4. Access individual profiles

### Team Member Profiles

Click on any team member to view:
- Personal information
- Contact details
- Employment history
- Current role and responsibilities
- Performance history

### Attendance Alerts

Receive notifications for:
- Late arrivals
- Unplanned absences
- Missing clock-out

### Requesting Attendance Corrections

If a team member had technical issues:
1. Go to **Team > Attendance > Corrections**
2. Click **Request Correction**
3. Select employee and date
4. Provide justification
5. Submit to HR

## Leave Approval

### Reviewing Leave Requests

1. Navigate to **Leave > Pending**
2. See request details:
   - Employee name
   - Leave type
   - Dates requested
   - Duration
   - Reason
   - Current balance
3. Check team calendar for conflicts

### Approving Leaves

1. Review request thoroughly
2. Check team coverage
3. Options:
   - **Approve** - Grant the leave
   - **Reject** - Deny with clear reason
   - **Defer** - Request alternative dates

### Team Leave Calendar

View **Leave > Calendar** to:
- See who's on leave
- Plan team coverage
- Avoid scheduling conflicts
- Ensure adequate staffing

### Best Practices for Leave Approval

- Respond within 24-48 hours
- Be consistent with policies
- Consider team workload
- Communicate clearly
- Plan for coverage

## Performance Management

### Conducting Performance Reviews

1. Navigate to **Performance > My Team**
2. Click on pending review
3. Complete evaluation:
   - Rate KPIs
   - Provide specific examples
   - Highlight strengths
   - Identify areas for improvement
   - Set new goals
4. Submit review

### Setting Goals

For each team member:
1. Go to **Performance > Goals**
2. Click **Set New Goal**
3. Define:
   - Goal description
   - Success criteria
   - Target date
   - Required resources
4. Save and share

### One-on-One Meetings

Track regular meetings:
- **Team > 1-on-1s**
- Schedule meetings
- Document discussions
- Track action items
- Follow up on progress

### Performance Feedback

Provide ongoing feedback:
1. Go to team member's profile
2. Click **Add Feedback**
3. Choose type (Positive/Constructive)
4. Enter details
5. Save (visible to employee and HR)
